Paid Leave Allocation & Allowance Rates (T008 Table)

A certain portion of any paid leave (Vacation, Stat, etc) is attributed to the increase (decrease) in total hours worked. The percentage of the change in hours worked that is attributed to paid absences is a fixed rate found in the T008 table.

These rates are multiplied by the YOY Hours Worked (Time Group Hr Wk) to yield the Leave Hours Worked columns in the DLM. This amount is also subtracted from the straight Absence Hours. This gives a clearer picture of the YOY paid leave by showing only change in behaviour related to absences and not a change in the base hours worked.

The table gives the rates per Act Group, and per GLCE Group Name (see below). The T110 also uses the same Act Sub Group.

This table also contains the Allowances rates, which are values that when mulitplied by the change in hours worked, yields a dollar impact.

The adjustments are filterable by:

  • ActGroup
  • GLCEGrp
  • Allocation Time Type
