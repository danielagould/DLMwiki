Plant and Non Plant Split

All MP Act Group hours and dollars are separated into Plant and Non Plant. This distinction is important as it differentiates MP hours in depots from those in large mail processing plants.

The split is done by cost centre, with some all the major processing plant cost centres identified as an input into the DLM model. All MP hours/dollars associated with these plant cost centres are rolled up as MP Plant. All remaining MP hours/dollars are rolled up as MP Non Plant.
