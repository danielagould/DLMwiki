GLCE Group (Hours)

The GLCE Group is the hour type for each hour work, whether Full Time (FT), Part Time (PT), Temporary (TMP) or Overtime (OT).

For the Time Group Hours Worked (Hr Wk) the GLCE Group is determined using the tblTimeCode table mapping (see the Time Group methodology).

For the non Hours Worked Time Groups the Employee SubGroup is used to map the hours to a GLCE Grp. This info is found in the tblEESubGrp table (see below). The Employee SubGroup is a field available in the 9502 and is pulled into the DLM (see the Hours methodology)
