Last Year's Paid Rate

The Last Year's Paid Rate is a dollar/hour rate used to calculate the labour impact in the current year without the impact of any wage increases. By multiplying both this year's hours and last years hours by the same rate, impacts due to changes in rate are removed (and can be calculated later). Using this logic, the rate that was used last year is calculated and used for various calculations in the DLM.

The calculated Last Year's Paid Rate can be found in the T313 - LY Paid Rate table, which is outputted by the DLM for reference.

Value = SUMOF[Dollars] / SUMOF[Hours]

Where Dollars and Hours are summed by Period, GLCEGrp, and ActGroup.
The Dollars are Hours Related Only (this distinction is accomplished via the GLCEGrp).
