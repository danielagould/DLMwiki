Adjustments

Adjustments are entered into the T110 Excel Workbook by the regional advisors and are used by the DLM to attribute impacts not presented in BW. These include one time transfers of hours or dollars, restructures, day count adjustments and other idiosyncratic impacts.

The T110 contains both an hours tab and dollars tab, both of which are used by the DLM.

The T110 also uses the same Act Sub Group, Time Group and GLCE Group as the DLM, so no mapping is necessary when it is used by the DLM.

The T110 uses a series of Drivers and SubDrivers, which the DLM uses to assign and map the impacts (see below).

The adjustments are filterable by:

  • Hours
  • Dollars
  • ActSubGroup
  • Plant
  • Driver
  • SubDriver
