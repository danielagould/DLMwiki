Dollars Hours vs Non Hours Split

All dollar data in the DLM is labour related but some of it is not directly tied to a payable hour (either worked or some for of paid absence). Dollars directly tied to a paid hour are referred to as Hours Related and all other dollars are Non Hours Related.

Examples of non hours related entries are: Boot & Glove, Rest Period, and various Allowances.

The mapping is done using the T002 - GLCE table in the DLM, which maps each GLCEGrp to either Hours Related or Non Hours Related.
